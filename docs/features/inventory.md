# Inventory Management

Track and manage your product inventory.

![Inventory Management](/images/inventory.png)

## Overview

- Real-time stock tracking
- Product management
- Low stock alerts
- Category organization
- Inventory valuation

## Product Management

### Adding Products

Required fields:

- Product name
- Price
- Initial stock quantity
- Category

Optional fields:

- Description
- Low stock threshold
- Product images
- GTIN barcode

### Editing Products

- Update pricing, descriptions, and categories
- Adjust stock levels
- Modify low stock thresholds

## Stock Management

### Stock Tracking

- Real-time stock levels
- Automatic updates on sales
- Stock movement history

### Deshelving (Stock Adjustments)

Remove inventory for:

- **Damaged** - Items damaged in store
- **Returned** - Customer returns
- **Expired** - Expired products
- **Reserved** - Reserved items
- **Other** - Other reasons

Each deshelving records quantity, reason, and optional description for audit purposes.

### Stock Alerts

- Configurable low stock threshold per product
- Dashboard notifications
- Low stock bell icon with count

## Categories

Predefined categories:

- Electronics, Clothing, Food & Beverages, Home & Garden, Office Supplies, Other

## Search & Filtering

- Search by product name (fuzzy search)
- Filter by category
- Filter by stock status (In Stock, Low Stock, Out of Stock)

## Inventory Reports

- View all products with quantities
- Total inventory value
- Low stock items highlighted
- Export to CSV

## Sales Integration

- Stock automatically decreases on sale
- Real-time synchronization
- Audit trail for all changes

## AI-Powered Insights

Get intelligent recommendations for:

- Reorder quantities and timing
- Slow-moving item actions
- Inventory optimization

## Access Control

Role-based permissions:

- **Admin** - Full inventory control
- **Manager** - View and edit products
- **Cashier** - View-only access

## Audit Trail

All inventory changes are logged with:

- User who made the change
- What was changed
- Timestamp
- Reason (for deshelvings)
